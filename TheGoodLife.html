About Us

At The Good Life, we understand that your pets are family. That's why we provide personalized, loving care for your furry friends and peace of mind for you while you're away.

With over 10 years of experience in pet care and house sitting, we treat every home and pet with the same attention and care we'd want for our own.

Bonded & Insured Certified Pet First Aid References Available

Our Services

In-Home Pet Sitting

Your pets stay in the comfort of their own home while receiving personalized care, including feeding, playtime, and lots of love.

  • Daily visits or overnight stays
  • Medication administration
  • Litter box/yard cleanup

Pet Training

Professional training services to help your pet learn good manners and basic commands in a positive, reward-based environment.

  • Basic obedience training
  • Leash walking skills
  • Behavioral modification
  • Puppy socialization

Medical Care & Exercise

Specialized care for pets with medical needs and customized exercise programs to keep your pet healthy and happy.

  • Medication administration
  • Subcutaneous fluids
  • Physical therapy exercises
  • Senior pet care

Additional Services

House Sitting

We'll take care of your home while you're away - mail collection, plant care, light cleaning, and security checks.

Transportation

Need your pet taken to the vet or groomer? We provide safe, reliable transportation services.

Photo Updates

Daily photo and text updates so you can see how your pets are doing while you're away.
